TELECOM RA AGENTIC POC
System Mapping Context Reference
Version: Demo v3

Purpose
-------
This file is context for the Design Agent.
It does not contain completed control logic, SQL, or prescribed exception rules.

Systems
-------
One Siebel:
- CRM, order capture and service inventory system.
- Tables: siebel_accounts, siebel_orders, siebel_assets.

Antillia:
- Billing system.
- Tables: antillia_accounts, antillia_products.

One Siebel Relationships
------------------------
siebel_accounts:
- Customer account master.
- Useful fields: account_id, account_name, account_type, region, status, created_date.
- Related to siebel_orders by account_id.
- Related to siebel_assets by account_id.

siebel_orders:
- Order activity.
- Useful fields: order_id, account_id, asset_id, order_date, order_type, quantity, unit_price, total_price, order_status.
- Valid order statuses: Completed, In Progress, Open.
- Related to siebel_accounts by account_id.
- Related to siebel_assets by asset_id.

siebel_assets:
- Service inventory.
- Useful fields: asset_id, account_id, asset_type, installation_date, asset_status, location, maintenance_cost, service_number.
- Valid asset statuses: Active, Inactive, Ceased.
- Related to siebel_accounts by account_id.
- Related to siebel_orders by asset_id.

Antillia Relationships
----------------------
antillia_accounts:
- Billing account.
- Useful fields: billing_account_id, account_id, billing_address, payment_method, billing_cycle, currency, status, created_date, service_number.
- Valid statuses: Active, Inactive, Ceased.
- Related to antillia_products by billing_account_id.
- May relate to One Siebel by account_id and/or service_number.

antillia_products:
- Billable product or charge.
- Useful fields: billing_product_id, billing_account_id, asset_id, order_id, product_name, charge_type, charge_amount, last_billed_date, next_billing_date, status.
- Valid statuses: Active, Inactive, Ceased.
- Related to antillia_accounts by billing_account_id.
- May relate to One Siebel by asset_id and/or order_id.

Possible Cross-System Relationship Fields
-----------------------------------------
Service-level:
- siebel_assets.service_number
- antillia_accounts.service_number

Asset-level:
- siebel_assets.asset_id
- antillia_products.asset_id

Account-level:
- siebel_accounts.account_id
- siebel_assets.account_id
- siebel_orders.account_id
- antillia_accounts.account_id

Order-level:
- siebel_orders.order_id
- antillia_products.order_id

Product comparison:
- siebel_assets.asset_type
- antillia_products.product_name

Status comparison:
- siebel_accounts.status
- siebel_assets.asset_status
- antillia_accounts.status
- antillia_products.status

Telecom Vocabulary
------------------
Common products:
- PSTN Line
- Broadband
- FTTP Broadband
- FTTC Broadband
- SIP Trunk
- Ethernet Circuit
- MPLS Circuit
- Cloud Voice
- Mobile Voice
- SD-WAN

Business terms:
- SNB means Service No Bill.
- BNS means Bill No Service.
- Completeness control checks whether expected records exist across systems.
- Accuracy control checks whether matched records have consistent values.

Expected Agent Behaviour
------------------------
Design Agent:
- Understands user objective.
- Selects relevant systems and tables.
- Selects possible relationship fields.
- Proposes design and assumptions.
- Waits for approval.

Developer Agent:
- Runs only after design approval.
- Converts approved design into BigQuery SQL.
- Executes control.
- Returns exception records.
- Waits for approval.

Insight Agent:
- Runs only after control output approval.
- Summarises findings.
- Creates downloadable insights.
- Presents insights in Streamlit.
